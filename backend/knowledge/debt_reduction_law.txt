DEBT REDUCTION AND SETTLEMENT FRAMEWORK
========================================

This knowledge base contains general information about debt reduction
programs and legal frameworks. This is placeholder content for Phase 1.

GENERAL PRINCIPLES:
- Debts that have been past due for extended periods may be eligible
  for significant reduction under applicable law.
- Creditors often prefer to recover a portion of the debt rather than
  pursue costly legal proceedings.
- Settlement programs typically offer reductions between 30% and 70%
  of the original balance depending on age and type of debt.

DEBTOR RIGHTS:
- Right to request verification of the debt.
- Right to dispute inaccurate information.
- Right to be treated with respect and dignity.
- Right to request communication in writing.
- Right to end a phone conversation at any time.
- Protection against harassment or abusive collection practices.

SETTLEMENT PROCESS:
1. Verification of debt details.
2. Assessment of debtor's financial situation.
3. Presentation of available settlement options.
4. Negotiation of terms.
5. Formal agreement documentation.
6. Payment processing.
7. Credit report update upon completion.
